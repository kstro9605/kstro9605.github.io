Summary

Kyle Strother is a hardworking, dedicated Software Engineer experienced with multiple programming languages and tools.

He is currently employed at Lockheed Martin. Within this role, Kyle has had the opportunity to interact with customer stake holders, collaborate with cross-functional groups from different departments such as integration, testing, and information assurance; and on multiple occasions perform tasks outside of his regularly expected duties with success and willingness to adapt to ever changing requirements. Among these are his experiences in producing a program specific Windows installation delivered by both hard drive delivery as well as .ISO disc delivery, maintaining up to date versioning of COTS (Commercial off-the-shelf) products such as Adobe, Google Chrome, 7Zip, Oracle java installations, Windows, and Exchange Server software.

Kyle is interested in advancing his development skills on multiple fronts, and honing his general programming abilities further with excitement and passion. He is striving to utilize his full stack development experiences and familiarity with container technologies, and front end development frameworks to solve increasingly complex problems.

Professionally; Kyle has worked with Java, Python, C#, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Docker containers, and Windows and Linux Operating systems, as well as their respective scripting frameworks. Kyle has also been working on developing his skill-set with the React Framework as well as the .NET framework. He has also dabbled with Type Script, Kotlin, and has a budding curiosity for cloud based frameworks such as AWS, and Azure.

During an internship at Welkin Sciences Kyle also gained experience working with embedded programming and rudimentary hardware tasks such as testing electric potential difference across components and minor soldering tasks involving custom designed circuit boards and FPGAs.

Kyle graduated from UCCS in 2020, with a bachelors with special honors in Computer Science, a minor in mathematics, and a certificate in Homeland Security. Since his graduation, Kyle has persued continuing education in the form of completing certification courses from the University of Michigan's Coursera specialization in Web development with focus on HTML5, CSS3, and Interactivity with JavaScript.
